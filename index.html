About Borderline Events

Based in the gorgeous Waipu, Bream Bay area we have over 40 years of experience in the music industry, promotion, event management and technical production. Focusing on developing music events in Bream Bay, Waipu and surrounding areas over the summer months. and as we grow, the goal is to expand our horizons and start producing indoor events in the winter.

GIVING BACK!

Charities & Local Club Support

When we moved to Waipu, my wife and I decided we really wanted to be part of the local community and be able to give back as much as we get from enjoying living here.

The Lions Club, Waipu Surf lifesaving Club, Waipu Volunteer Fire Brigade and the Waipu Hospice are just some of the charity's and clubs we support with proceeds from our events.

OUR COMPANY OBJECTIVES

Providing safe, FUN and affordable entertainment in Northland

Northland in summer is a hive of activity, with many of the surrounding camp grounds at capacity over January and February. The beaches are beautiful the sand is white and the ocean is warm so there is plenty to do when the sun is out!

Once the sun goes down the options for entertainment become less abundant. Waipu township boasts the world famous Pizza Barn and McLeods Brewery, and Waipu Cove has "The Cove" Restaurant both with award winning service and food. However live entertainment is not part of these establishments agenda so in the area live music is fairly limited.

THATS WHERE WE COME IN!

Our Stunning Venue

Tahamoana Farm - Where Every Event Becomes Unforgettable

Nestled on the picturesque Cove Road in Waipu, Tahamoana Farm offers breathtaking views over the Hen and Chicks Islands and the pristine waters of Bream Bay. The versatile outdoor venue seamlessly blends natural beauty with thoughtful design, creating the perfect backdrop for any celebration.

Whether you're planning an intimate wedding ceremony, a milestone birthday celebration, a corporate event, or a large-scale concert, our venue adapts to your vision. The natural amphitheater setting provides excellent acoustics and sightlines, while multiple viewing areas ensure every guest has the perfect vantage point.
